Boarding Facility

A Safe Haven for Your Furry Friends

Our boarding facility offers a cool, fully ventilated environment with individual kennels that open into a nature-like play area. We prioritize your pet's comfort and safety, ensuring a stress-free experience 24/7.

    with 24/7 fresh air ventilation.
  • Nature-Like Play Area

  • Night Monitoring

Every day is packed with fun! From private play sessions to group activities for compatible dogs, we ensure your pet stays active and happy. Guests typically sleep soundly through the night, dreaming of their day's adventures.

All Boarding Stays Include

Standard amenities for every guest

Play Time

Supervised group fun for compatible, well-behaved dogs. It's a great way to socialize! (Pets must be spayed/neutered to join).
