Our Story

From a simple belief to a trusted community.

The Need

Many pet owners worried about finding a reliable lodging facility, fearing their pets wouldn't be safe or comfortable while they were away on vacation or work.

The Solution

Dr. Onkar Pawaskar created PetsHotel to bridge this gap. With 9 spacious individual kennels and a play area, we offer a professional, hygienic environment managed by veterinary experts.

The Promise

We ensure your pet enjoys their stay with ample space to stretch and exercise. Our loving hospitality staff looks after their health, nutrition, and happiness, so they look forward to coming back!
